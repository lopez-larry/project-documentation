- **Project Name:** Apollo Website Redesign
- **Project Manager:** Sarah Chen
- **Start Date:** 2026-05-01
- **End Date:** 2026-07-31
- **Objective:** Redesign and modernize the corporate website
- **Scope Summary:** UI/UX redesign, development, hosting setup, and deployment

---

## 2. Procurement Strategy
- **Procurement Approach:** Buy (external vendors for specialized tools and assets)
- **Contract Types:** 
  - Fixed Price (design assets)
  - Subscription (software tools)
- **Vendor Selection Strategy:** Competitive bidding for assets, existing vendors for tools
- **Number of Vendors:** Multiple
- **Global vs Local Sourcing:** Global (digital assets), Local (if needed for support)

## 4. Vendor Selection Criteria
| Criteria        | Weight (%) | Description |
|-----------------|------------|-------------|
| Cost            | 30%        | Competitive pricing |
| Quality         | 25%        | Product/service reliability |
| Experience      | 20%        | Proven track record |
| Delivery Time   | 15%        | Speed of delivery |
| Compliance      | 10%        | Security/legal standards |

## 5. Procurement Process
- **Step 1:** Define requirements (design assets, hosting, tools)
- **Step 2:** Issue RFQ for stock images and icon packages
- **Step 3:** Evaluate vendors (price, quality, licensing terms)
- **Step 4:** Negotiate pricing and usage rights
- **Step 5:** Award contracts / purchase subscriptions
- **Step 6:** Monitor vendor performance and usage

## 7. Contract Management Plan
- **Contract Type per Vendor:**
  - Subscription (Figma, AWS)
  - Fixed Price (stock assets)
- **Key Terms & Conditions:**
  - Licensing rights for images/icons
  - Usage limitations
- **SLAs (Service Level Agreements):**
  - AWS uptime: 99.9%
- **Payment Terms:**
  - Monthly (subscriptions)
  - One-time (assets)
- **Penalties / Incentives:**
  - SLA credits for downtime

---

## 8. Vendor Management
- **Communication Plan:** Email + weekly check-ins for critical vendors
- **Performance Reviews:** Monthly review for AWS usage/performance
- **Escalation Process:** Vendor support → Account manager → Internal escalation
- **Reporting Frequency:** Monthly

---

## 9. Risk Management (Procurement)
| Risk                          | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|-------------------------------|--------|------------|------------|
| Vendor delay (assets)         | Medium | Medium     | Order early |
| Licensing issues              | High   | Low        | Legal review |
| AWS downtime                  | High   | Low        | Multi-region backup |
| Cost overruns (subscriptions) | Medium | Medium     | Monitor usage |

---

## 10. Change Management
- **Change Request Process:** Submit via project change log
- **Approval Authority:** Project Manager + Sponsor
- **Contract Modification Process:** Amend contract or re-negotiate terms
