## 2. Budgeting Approach
- **Estimation Method:** Bottom-up (based on resource hours and vendor costs)
- **Cost Baseline Method:** Aggregated from CBS and approved by sponsor
- **Tools Used:** Excel, Jira
- **Assumptions:**
  - 8-hour workdays
  - No major scope changes
- **Constraints:**
  - Fixed budget cap: $120,000

## 7. Cost Control Plan
- **Tracking Method:** Earned Value Management (EVM)
- **Reporting Frequency:** Weekly
- **Variance Thresholds:**
  - ±10% triggers review
- **Escalation Process:**
  - PM → Sponsor if threshold exceeded

---

## 8. Performance Measurement
- **KPIs:**
  - Cost Variance (CV)
  - Schedule Variance (SV)
  - Cost Performance Index (CPI)
- **Tools Used:** Jira dashboards, Excel tracking

---

## 9. Risk Management (Budget)
| Risk                          | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|-------------------------------|--------|------------|------------|
| Labor overrun                 | High   | Medium     | Track weekly |
| Vendor cost increase          | Medium | Low        | Fixed contracts |
| Scope creep                   | High   | Medium     | Strict change control |

---

## 10. Change Management
- **Change Request Process:** Submit via Jira
- **Approval Authority:** PM + Sponsor
- **Budget Adjustment Rules:** Use contingency first, then request additional funding
