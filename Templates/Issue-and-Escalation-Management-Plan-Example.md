## 2. Issue Management Approach
- **Definition of Issue:** Any active problem impacting timeline, cost, scope, or quality
- **Tools Used:** Jira (issue tracking), Slack (communication), Confluence (documentation)
- **Tracking Method:** Centralized issue log in Jira
- **Assumptions:**
  - All issues must be logged within 24 hours
- **Constraints:**
  - Limited escalation bandwidth from sponsor

## 4. Issue Classification
| Severity Level | Description                                      | Example |
|----------------|--------------------------------------------------|---------|
| Low            | Minor impact, no schedule effect                 | UI alignment issue |
| Medium         | Moderate impact, small delay                     | Design delay |
| High           | Significant impact, may delay project            | API failure |
| Critical       | Project at risk, requires immediate action       | System outage |

---

## 5. Issue Resolution Process
- **Step 1:** Issue logged in Jira
- **Step 2:** Assigned to responsible owner
- **Step 3:** Severity assessed by PM
- **Step 4:** Resolution plan created
- **Step 5:** Fix implemented by team
- **Step 6:** QA validates resolution
- **Step 7:** Issue closed

## 7. Escalation Criteria
| Condition                          | Escalation Level |
|-----------------------------------|------------------|
| Issue unresolved > 48 hours       | Escalate to PM   |
| High severity issue               | Immediate escalation to Sponsor |
| Critical system failure           | Escalate to Steering Committee |
| Budget or timeline risk > 10%     | Escalate to Sponsor |

---

## 8. Escalation Path
- **Level 1:** Team Lead
- **Level 2:** Project Manager
- **Level 3:** Project Sponsor
- **Level 4:** Steering Committee

---

## 9. Escalation Process
- **Step 1:** Trigger identified (e.g., high severity issue)
- **Step 2:** Notify next escalation level via Slack/email
- **Step 3:** Provide issue summary:
  - Description
  - Impact (cost, schedule)
  - Proposed solution
- **Step 4:** Decision made within defined SLA
- **Step 5:** Document outcome in issue log

---

## 10. Communication Plan (Issues & Escalations)
- **Stakeholders Notified:** PM, Sponsor, impacted teams
- **Communication Method:**
  - Slack (urgent issues)
  - Email (formal updates)
  - Weekly status report
- **Frequency:**
  - Immediate (critical)
  - Weekly (standard reporting)
- **Reporting Format:** Dashboard + summary report

---

## 11. Monitoring & Reporting
- **Tracking Method:** Jira dashboard
- **KPIs:**
  - Average resolution time (< 3 days target)
  - Number of open issues
  - Escalation rate (< 10%)
- **Reporting Frequency:** Weekly
